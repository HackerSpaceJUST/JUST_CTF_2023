About

HackerSpace_JUST is a community of individuals who share a passion for cybersecurity. Originally, the activities of HackerSpace_JUST were geared towards students of JUST (Jordan University of Science and Technology), but the community has since expanded to include students from all universities as well as professional individuals from Jordan. The community offers a variety of activities to help individuals improve their cybersecurity skills, including organizing and providing cybersecurity courses and sessions, as well as hosting cybersecurity CTF (Capture the Flag) competitions. These opportunities allow members to gain hands-on experience and knowledge in the field, and also provide an environment for individuals to showcase their skills and engage with others who share similar interests.
The fourth edition of JUST CTF will be held on February 22nd, 2023 at JUST campus. Participants can form teams of 2 to 4 members to compete in challenges fitting all skill levels. Get ready for an exciting and informative competition!
